NAMDOL'S JOURNEY

"As a Tibetan-American, I've had the privilege to draw on the best of both worlds."

At 17, after visiting family in Ladakh, Namdol founded Zampa to bridge the gap between Western comforts and traditional wisdom. Through teaching English, playing the dranyen, and sharing stories, the mission is to empower youth with purposeful living.
