WHAT IS ZAMPA

Building Bridges Between Two Worlds

Zampa (Tibetan for "bridge") connects young adults from the United States and the Himalayas through mutual learning, dignity-based exchange, and youth-driven storytelling. It's not charity—it's kinship.
